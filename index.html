About College :

Overview :

  • Trinity College of Engineering and Research, Pune, is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development.

  • Established with a vision to nurture future leaders in engineering and technology, we provide students with a dynamic learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

  • Focuses on fostering strong industry-academia partnerships for better career opportunities.

  • Features state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and a vibrant campus culture.

Missions :

  • To deliver quality education through industry-oriented programs and experienced faculty.

  • To promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship that contribute to societal and industrial advancements.

  • Establish strong collaborations with: Global industries and Research institutions

  • Enable students to engage in real-world projects and develop cutting-edge solutions.

  • Encourage students to actively participate in hackathons, conferences, and seminars.
